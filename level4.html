IV. THE SNOW FORTRESS

This is Eames's dream. Eames is an essential part of the secrecy and deceit within the dream world.

Cobb, Ariadne, Saito, Eames and Fischer go into this level. Arthur stays back to initiate, and more importantly synchronize the kick.

Fischer must be taken to the Fort, where the idea they wish to plant in his mind finally takes hold. Mal(projection of Cobb's subconscious) kills Fischer. Fischer's dead, so he's gone into limbo.

Eames blows up the support of the fortress,dropping it and causing a freefall. Saito, who is close to dying, helps Eames in his last moments by warding off the enemies.
