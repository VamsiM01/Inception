V. LIMBO

There is no dreamer here, it is a shared state. It is infinite space of nothing. But if a person already was in limbo (Cobb),his dream space is present.

Cobb and Ariadne go into limbo to bring Fischer back. Saito is also dead, and hence is also in limbo.

Cobb knew inception is possible because he first tried it on Mal. He makes a fake deal with Mal, to get Fischer. Fischer and Ariadne go back to the fortress, Cobb stays back to bring Saito.

Ariadne and Fischer fall off the building. Cobb finds Saito, and the both shoot themselves to come back.
