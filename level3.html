III. THE HOTEL

This is Arthur's dream. Arthur is Cobb's right-hand man.

Cobb, Arthur, Ariadne, Saito, Eames, and Fischer go into this level. Yusuf stays behind to initiate the kick.

Fischer is tricked into believing Browning(his godfather) is a traitor. So, to find out the truth that Browning was hiding, he joins the team(willingly).

Arthur plants bombs in the hotel room, but Yusuf's kick was too early. There's no gravity, since the van is in freefall. So, Arthur had to improvise. He moved everyone to the elevator, and simulated a freefall by planting charges.
