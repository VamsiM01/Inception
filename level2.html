II. THE VAN CHASE

This is Yusuf's dream. He is the chemist, who formulated chemical compounds strong enough to go 3 dream levels deep.

Cobb, Arthur, Ariadne, Eames, Saito, Yusuf go into the dream, taking Fischer along with them.

Fischer is kidnapped. Cobb forces Fischer to give him random numbers, which will be used in the next level. They begin planting the idea in his head that his father wants him to break up the company.

Yusuf drives the van off the bridge, but it was too early. A second kick occurs when the van hits the water.
